Lead Capture & URL Extraction Test

Simulates the chatbot being on different university/course pages. Captures a lead and verifies that the correct university and product/course are extracted from the URL and sent to the CRM.

1 Load the Widget

2 Simulate a Page URL

Pick a URL to simulate. The widget will behave as if the visitor is on that page — the server will run AI URL extraction on it when a lead is captured.

3 Chat, Capture a Lead & Verify

1

Open the chat widget

Click the chat bubble that appeared in the bottom-right corner after loading the widget.

2

Simulate a URL first

Pick a preset from Section 2 above and click Simulate This URL before or during the conversation. The widget immediately starts using it.

3

Provide your contact details in chat

Share your name and phone number (and optionally email). The bot will capture these as a lead.

4

Watch the server logs

In the Replit console, look for lines like:
[URL Extraction] AI extracted: university="IIM Ahmedabad" product="MBA"
or [URL Extraction] Cache HIT for: ... if the URL was already processed before.

5

Verify in LeadSquared

Open LeadSquared and find the new lead. Check that the university and product/course fields contain the expected values for the URL you simulated.

6

Test cache vs. fresh AI extraction

The first time a URL is processed, AI is called. After that, results are cached in the database. To force a fresh AI call, use a slightly different URL (e.g. add a trailing slash or query param) or clear the cache table leadsquared_url_extraction_cache.
